I'm a Blue Tick Hound. One cold Duluth morning these guys find me frozen and put me in a car and take me to a ship. They thawed me out and swore me in. I ate with the crew. There were no trees, obviously. But many things looked like fire hydrants. I stood watch on the bow and my job was to bark if I saw rocks. I'm 140 in dog years.
